# Circular Motion

Dynamics of objects moving in circular paths.

## Concepts
- Centripetal acceleration: a = v²/r
- Centripetal force: F = mv²/r
- Banking of roads

## Examples
- Car turning on curved road
- Satellite orbits
- Amusement park rides
