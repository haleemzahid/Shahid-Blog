# Projectile Motion

Analysis of two-dimensional motion under gravity.

## Key Formulas
- Range: R = (v₀² sin2θ) / g
- Maximum Height: H = (v₀² sin²θ) / 2g
- Time of flight: T = (2v₀ sinθ) / g

## Applications
Basketball, cannon fire, water fountains.
