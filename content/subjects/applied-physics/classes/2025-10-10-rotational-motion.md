# Rotational Motion Basics

Introduction to rotational kinematics and dynamics.

## Angular Quantities
- Angular displacement: θ (radians)
- Angular velocity: ω = dθ/dt
- Angular acceleration: α = dω/dt

## Torque
τ = r × F (cross product)

## Rotational Inertia
I = Σmr² (moment of inertia)
